What we do

Nutriseva is an initiative to address malnutrition in a more creative and contemporary way- by distributing at-home seed kits of superfoods and other nutritious plants

We distribute seed kits for growing many types of spinach. We aim to eradicate the issue of hidden malnutrition around the world.Malnutrtion can often go unnoticed and this makes it more of a problem to the society and the individual themselves .These seed kits contain the seeds of spinach that are easy growable and are extremely high in nutrition .We expect the receivers of these seeds to plant these seeds and harvest the spinach for daily use

Enabling a Healthier , Nutritious Lifestyle for everyone
